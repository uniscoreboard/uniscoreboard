Meet our Team

...our one-man team.

Uniscoreboard was created by graphic designer and NFL fanatic Matt Cronin with one simple goal: to provide NFL fans one clean, straightforward place to see all the latest scores—no extra clicking, no ads, no juggling between multiple tabs. Whether you’re catching up during the offseason or following the current season’s action, Uniscoreboard keeps everything in one easy-to-access spot.

The project began shortly after the 2025 Super Bowl, inspired by Matt’s desire to relive the last few seasons of NFL action without the clutter and distractions often found on other sports sites. From that initial spark, Uniscoreboard grew into a seamless scoreboard experience that covers multiple seasons, all presented clearly and efficiently.

Uniscoreboard is designed for fans who want quick, no-nonsense access to NFL scores—because following your favorite sport should be simple and enjoyable.
